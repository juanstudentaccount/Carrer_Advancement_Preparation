Team collaboration and communication strategies
üìë Source: Project Presentation
Topic: Team collaboration and communication strategies
Situation
In my project management class, I had a group presentation on IT Procurement and Governance where we were all tasked with delivering a presentation explaining the concept.
Task
Our team was tasked with delivering a presentation on IT procurement and governance. The presentation required various sections which included career exploration, professional interviews, and an interactive quiz, and in the end, slides that packaged all the sections into one presentation.
Action
Communication was key since we all needed to collaborate on which parts we were going to get done. I was tasked with conducting two interviews with IT professionals. During my search, I used various websites to reach out, some websites I used were LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor. Unfortunately, it took me a long time to conduct my interviews since many IT professionals did not reach out. I communicated this with my team, and they were very understanding of my situation.
Result
Since my team was understanding and we all communicated in a professional manner, this allowed me more time to continue to reach out, and in the end, the result was positive because I received two emails from people that were interested in having an interview with me. In the end, I submitted my part on time and we presented our presentation on time. This highlights how communication is important and can result in positive outcomes when working in teams.

Build quality into processes and deliverables
üìò Source: PMI Principle 8 ‚Äì Build quality into processes and deliverables
Topic: Build quality into processes and deliverables
Situation
For my semester-long project, one of my deliverables was a project charter that outlined my goals and a plan on how to achieve those goals. The project charter also had other detailed sections explaining stakeholders, costs, and a methodology section.
Task
I was tasked to design and build the site, and the goal was not to just complete it but rather produce a high-quality project that had clear writing and a clean layout with links and different sections. Finding valuable deliverables was a challenge because I had a hard time finding deliverables that would benefit me and provide myself value.

I began by drafting different ideas that would provide me with value, and at first I picked to make a research project, but after receiving feedback, I realized this project was not viable. In the end, I created a project that had clear deliverables with clear steps, and I made sure that every deliverable provided me with value in my professional career.
Result
I created a solid and polished project charter that included deliverables and clear steps on how to achieve them. The project charter was a success, and what helped me the most was focusing on the quality of my work and building structured plans on how to execute them.
Navigate complexity
üìò Source: PMI Principle 9 ‚Äì Navigate complexity
Topic: Navigate complexity in technical tasks and projects
Situation
During one of my AWS labs, I was working on a long lab that expected me to figure out the process of creating a working server architecture. During my lab, it timed out since it took me hours, and all my progress was deleted.
Task
In one of my CIS classes, I was tasked with completing several labs showing me the AWS architecture and features. At the end, I was tasked to show my understanding on how to build a working architecture with several features. During my attempt, I got very close, but I was stuck on one requirement and I could not find the correct solution to get full credit. After many hours, the lab timed out and all my progress was deleted.
Action
I was very frustrated because I spent several hours working on my lab and all my progress had been deleted since the lab timed out. The next step I took was to calm down and focus on what I did right. I wrote down steps to make sure I configured everything correctly, and after troubleshooting, I found out how to set everything correctly.
Result
After writing a structured plan on what steps to take, I navigated a complex topic and troubleshot all the issues I had. This situation highlights the importance of staying calm and approaching complex topics with a plan to help navigate complex issues.
Be a diligent, respectful, and caring steward
üìò Source: PMI Principle 1 ‚Äì Be a diligent, respectful, and caring steward
Topic: Acting as a respectful and caring steward for your team
Situation
During one of my previous classes at CSULA, I had a project where team members were not ambitious and did not want to put effort into our project. I decided to be as respectful as possible and talk with my team members and motivate them to create a high-quality presentation.
Task
My group was tasked with creating a presentation on a nonprofit where we would draw diagrams and explain why implementing a system for the nonprofit would benefit people with difficulty receiving digital literacy and education. During our project, my team members seemed uninterested and wanted to do the minimal effort for the project.
Action
I decided to be as respectful as possible and motivate my team members to put more effort and dedicate more time to the project. I was very careful with not being a pushover, so instead I worked as much as possible on our project and helped my team members understand how to complete the project to assist them.
Result
In the end, because I kept a positive attitude, it spread towards the team, and in the end we presented a well-formatted presentation with all the required sections. Because of my positive attitude, I motivated and helped my team to put more effort into the project, and in the end we became friends.

Topic: Time management and milestone achievement
Situation
During my last semester at CSULA, I took very challenging classes that involved large workloads and complex topics. I learned so much during this semester and learned concepts and skills that I will continue to use in my life and professional career.
